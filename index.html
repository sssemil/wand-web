ABOUT WAND

Wand is a smart pen with temporary ink that sends to your smartphone every scratch you made on a piece of paper the way you wrote it. The temporary ink evaporates after about 30 minutes from the paper allowing you to see what you’ve written and reuse the same piece of paper aftewards while your data is stored nice and safe on your phone, and, if you want, backed up on our servers.
A pen may seem to be a small irrelevant thing but considering that only in the US 1.6 Billion pens are thrown away and 7 Billion trees are cut down for paper each year, our solution results in a tremendous impact on the climate change. An average tree can absorb 22kg of carbon dioxide per year. Through usage of our smartpen we save about 16.5 Million trees in 5 years and that reduces CO2 emission up to 3.5Mt.
